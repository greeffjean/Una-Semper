ABOUT

purpose driven humanbeing

I was born in Cape Town, South Africa, I love the Cape, it is without question my home and no other place on earth can replace this it. I am the way I am, and for that matter create the way I create not becuase of exposure or experience, or study - but because of my soul - spirit - life purpose.

My inner governs my expression and my expression cannot lie, that is what I love about it so much, its not other peoples truths that frightens me its my truth. When I express from deep within I feel like I am wielding a power allmighty that speaks powerfully and direct. I find immense purpose in artistic expression and without that purpose I would never have be propelled to create anything - the purpose I find in my expression is - sharing - the - messages - that - come - to - me - from - within - that - the world - needs - to - hear. Service to others gives me something that fills me.

There is still so much to learn about me that I don't yet know - I believe I need you, the reader, to assist me to remember all of who I am by participating in your own expression from deep within and hopefully sharing that with me.

Una Semper
